Football Fields in Veliko Tarnovo Province

Explore the football stadiums and fields in Veliko Tarnovo and the surrounding areas. Here are some of the notable fields:

  • Veliko Tarnovo Stadium
    Located in the heart of Veliko Tarnovo, this stadium is known for its vibrant atmosphere and local matches. It is home to the FC Yantra football team.
  • Gorna Oryahovitsa Stadium
    Situated in Gorna Oryahovitsa, this stadium hosts various football events and is a key venue for local sports. It serves as the home ground for the PFC Lokomotiv Gorna Oryahovitsa.
  • Lyaskovets Football Field
    This field in Lyaskovets is used for local football matches and community sports events. It provides facilities for both training and competitive games.
  • Svishtov Football Field
    Located in Svishtov, this field is known for hosting local leagues and regional matches. It is an important facility for football enthusiasts in the area.
  • Elena Football Field
    The football field in Elena offers a scenic location for local matches and tournaments. It is a popular spot for community gatherings and sports events.
